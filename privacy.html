Privacy Policy

Home Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 15, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our n8n and AI automation services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: When you fill out our contact form or engage our services, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, business name, and phone number.
  • Project & System Information: To build your custom automations, we may require access to your business systems, APIs, and relevant data. This can include access credentials, which are always handled with the utmost security.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To design, build, and implement your custom n8n and AI automation workflows.
  • To communicate with you regarding your project, provide support, and respond to inquiries.
  • To process payments and manage your account.
  • To improve our website and service offerings.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Data Security and Confidentiality

We take the security of your data very seriously.

  • Confidentiality: All client information, including system credentials and business data, is treated as strictly confidential. We are happy to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before beginning any project.
  • Secure Storage: Any sensitive information, such as API keys or login credentials, is stored securely and accessed only for the purpose of developing and maintaining your automations.
  • Data Minimization: We only request access to the data and systems that are absolutely necessary to build your workflow.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With trusted third-party services (e.g., payment processors) who assist us in operating our business, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • To comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information that we hold. Upon termination of our services, we will securely remove any stored credentials or sensitive data related to your project.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@automationexperts.com.
